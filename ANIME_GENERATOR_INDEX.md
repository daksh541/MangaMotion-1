## ğŸ¯ Common Tasks

### Change Primary Color
1. Open `AnimeGenerator.tsx`
2. Find `from-purple-400 via-blue-400 to-pink-400`
3. Replace with your colors
4. Save and rebuild

### Add More Upload Slots
1. Find `.slice(0, 5)`
2. Change `5` to desired number
3. Save and rebuild

### Adjust Gallery Columns
1. Find `grid-cols-1 sm:grid-cols-2 lg:grid-cols-4`
2. Change `lg:grid-cols-4` to desired columns
3. Save and rebuild

### Speed Up Animations
1. Find `duration-300`
2. Change to `duration-200`
3. Save and rebuild
