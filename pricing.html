Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. You'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period.

What happens to my projects if I downgrade?

Your projects remain accessible, but you'll be limited to the features of your new plan. 4K projects will be available in 1080p.

Is there a free trial?

We offer a free plan that includes basic features. Paid plans don't have a trial period but can be cancelled anytime.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer refunds within 7 days of purchase if you're not satisfied with our service.
